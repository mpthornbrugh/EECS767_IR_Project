Search for a Course

Using the drop-down menus, select the location and school. Next, click the search button to view all credits we predict will transfer from that school. Use the Reset button to clear out all current selections.

Once your results are shown, you can use the search box to further narrow your results. Try searching for Subject, Course Number, or Course Title here.

If no results are shown for your search this means that a course has not been recently evaluated. Your coursework would be evaluated officially when the University of Kansas receives a copy of your official transcript.

List my selected courses

Check the box next to a course to remember this selection during your session. At any time you can click "List my selected courses" to view a list of the courses you have checked. You can also use this button to create and download a PDF of your selected courses.
