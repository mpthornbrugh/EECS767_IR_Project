Welcome to the Policy Office

By partnering with policy making units and individuals across all university units and departments, the KU Policy Office serves as an institution-wide resource for policy and policy development. Policy Office staff members facilitate the coordination of:

  • policy development,
  • policy approval,
  • and periodic policy review and revision.

Members of the Policy Office serve as resource specialists on the process by which policies are created, revised, and published. As a service unit, we value collaboration with subject matter experts and strive to make each policy development process as inclusive as possible to ensure that policies are appropriate and serve our campus community.

The Policy Office is available to assist subject matter experts within units in determining the appropriate stakeholder groups and contacts who should be consulted during the creation or revision process. An important tool to support the transparency of development of policy is the process by which policy is developed, reviewed, and approved on the Lawrence campus.

Additionally, we are also responsible for maintenance and oversight of the Policy Library, which is the official university policy repository and serves as a resource for queries related to policies in place at KU.

For more information about the development and promulgation of policies at KU, including definitions of key terms such as policy and procedure, as well as articulation of responsibilities, please see the University Policy Program in the Policy Library or contact the Policy Office (785-864-9600 or policy@ku.edu).
