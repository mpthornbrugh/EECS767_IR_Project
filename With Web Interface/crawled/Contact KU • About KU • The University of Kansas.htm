Contact KU

The University of Kansas main campus is in Lawrence. We also have campuses in Kansas City, Overland Park, Wichita, and Salina, as well as research unit throughout the state.

Use the links below for an alphabetical listing of KU schools, departments, administrative units, organizations, and affiliates; to search for names and information; to see maps of and directions to the main campus; and for computing issues.
