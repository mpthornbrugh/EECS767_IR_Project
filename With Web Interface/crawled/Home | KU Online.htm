How to enroll »

KU's online courses and programs are a cost-effective convenient way to earn a top-notch education. If you are a student from another college looking to supplement your course work, a high school student looking to get a head start, or are a returning adult who would like to take a class for personal or professional enrichment, KU’s online course options can help you meet your educational goals.

Faculty/staff resources

The KU Center for Online and Distance Learning provides faculty services and support for online and hybrid learning. CODL staff are available to answer your questions and provide instructional design, media production, and expertise in design and delivery of courses.
