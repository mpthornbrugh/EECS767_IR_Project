Payroll Services serves the University’s diverse customer base by providing accurate and timely compensation to all staff, faculty and student employees in an efficient manner. We are committed to maintaining the most current understanding of Federal, State and University regulations regarding payroll in order to provide accurate information.

Announcements

 

 W-2 Information

The State of Kansas issues W-2's by mail or online.  The University of Kansas does not have access to print W-2's.

Online:
Log into the State of Kansas Self Service website and follow the online W-2 instructions.
 
By Mail:
2015 W-2 forms were mailed at the end of January 2016 to the address Human Resources has on file.  You can view or update your address online via your HR Pay Self-Service.
If you had previously given consent to receive your W-2 online, it would not have been mailed to you.
 
Duplicate Copy:
Send a request in writing to the Payroll Office or email payroll@ku.edu.  Your W-2 will be mailed by the State of Kansas within approximately 2 weeks.
Your request must include the following:
  • Full Name
  • Address (your W-2 will be mailed here)
  • Last 4 digits of your SSN
  • Year(s) of W-2 requested
