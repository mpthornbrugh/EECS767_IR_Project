Subscribe or
Copy & paste the following URL into your prefered feed reader http://www.news.ku.edu/headlines.xml

Really Simple Syndication (RSS) is an XML-based format for content distribution. KU updates its feed every weekday with press releases about KU.

How to access RSS feeds

To access an RSS feed, you need to use a feed reader also know known as an RSS/news aggregator. There are a number of free and commercial feed readers available. Simply search for RSS feed reader and select a product you like. Feed readers can be stand alone programs or a plugin to browers and email clients. Additionally, feed readers are incorporated into web pages such as Google's iGoogle and GMail, Yahoo's My Yahoo, and others.
